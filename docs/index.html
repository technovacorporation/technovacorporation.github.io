Company News & Milestones

January 2002: Technova launches its first broadband multimedia platform, "NovaNet," connecting over 10,000 users worldwide.

March 2002: Technova creates its final codename project for educational purposes, "CodenameBALDI" - designed to help disabled people or people with dementia learn. The system is innovative but still has some faults.

August 2003: Technova completes development of its own operating system, "Pendulum OS" - a fully proprietary OS designed entirely by Technova engineers. No Windows components are used. Pendulum OS powers Technova’s applications and provides a stable, secure platform for internal and educational apps.

March 2004: Their final creation, the Technova School, is unveiled - a comprehensive educational platform building on CodenameBALDI's experience and running on Pendulum OS.

Stay tuned for more updates - the future never stops evolving!
